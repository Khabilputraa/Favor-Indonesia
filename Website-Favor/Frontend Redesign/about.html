Our Story

Our Journey

Built from
Passion & Purpose

Favor Indonesia was born from a simple but powerful belief: that food has the power to connect people, preserve heritage, and build communities. Founded in the heart of Yogyakarta, we set out to create F&B experiences that go beyond just eating.

Today, our portfolio of brands spans artisan bakeries, premium patisseries, specialty coffee, express dining, and professional catering — each built with uncompromising standards and a deep respect for Indonesian culinary tradition.

Every product we create, every space we design, every cup we serve — carries the same DNA: quality you can taste, and hospitality you can feel.
